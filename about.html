About Xochi Gardens and Café

In the language of Nahuatl, Xochitl means Flower; The name of the Aztec goddess of love, beauty, and flowers, Xochiquetzal, derives from this word.


Inspired and named after Xochiquetzal, our mission at Xochi Gardens and Café is to share Mexican culture and the Earth's beauty by providing the people a serene and unforgetable experience within our private gardens and café.


With the help of our community, Xochi Gardens was created for visitors to decompress from the pressures of everyday life; and to experience a taste of Mexican culture. Designed by the hands of local Mexican and Mexican-American artisans, Xochi Gardens and Café has become known as a place to celebrate a cultural exchange; loved and treasured by locals and tourists alike.


Visit our festivals, host a celebration, relax under the shade, study with friends in our glass greenhouses, or step inside the café for a delicious meal; we hope to see you soon!
